Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Data Controller

This website is a personal portfolio managed by Umberto Di Laudo, based in Trieste, Italy. You can contact me at umbertodilaudo@gmail.com.

2. Data collected by this website

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data from visitors directly. There are no registration forms, no login systems, and no contact forms that submit data to a server.

The only data stored on your device is a theme preference (light or dark mode), saved in your browser's localStorage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible by the server.

3. Cloudflare Pages (hosting provider)

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, Cloudflare's static site hosting platform. Cloudflare is therefore both the hosting provider and the content delivery network (CDN). When you visit this site, your request is served directly by Cloudflare's global edge network, which may process your IP address, browser type, and request timestamps for the purpose of delivering content and protecting against malicious traffic.

Cloudflare processes this data as a data processor on my behalf, under its own Data Processing Addendum (DPA) compliant with GDPR — including EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international data transfers to the US. Cloudflare does not sell this data or use it for advertising purposes. For full details, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy .

The legal basis for this processing is legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): it is technically necessary to host and deliver the website and protect it from abuse.

4. Cookies

This website does not use cookies of any kind — no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, no tracking cookies. No cookie banner is displayed because no consent is required.

5. External links

This website contains links to external services (GitHub, LinkedIn, Google Scholar, academic repositories). Once you leave this site, their respective privacy policies apply. I have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

6. Fonts and libraries

All fonts (Inter, Playfair Display) and icon libraries (Lucide Icons) are self-hosted on this website. No data is sent to Google Fonts, unpkg, or any other external CDN when you visit this site.

7. Your rights under GDPR

Since this website does not collect personal data directly, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. For any data processed by Cloudflare's infrastructure, you may contact Cloudflare directly or refer to their privacy policy .

For any questions or requests, feel free to contact me at umbertodilaudo@gmail.com.
